Organization Description

Melody of Hope will partner with various non-profit organizations around the world. The goal of these partnerships is to raise funds while also becoming a voice and advocate for each charity’s cause. Our priorities and project funding will be based on the needs of the community that provide:

• support to low income families • health care
• educational opportunities • job training
• spiritual and mental counseling• support for wounded warriors
• economic development• resources to help end sex trafficking
• hunger relief
