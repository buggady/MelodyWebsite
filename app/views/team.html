MEET MELODY OF HOPE

Melody of Hope is an eclectic team of vibrant, youthful professionals inspired to change the world starting with North Texas. We are scattered across DFW, yet united under a singular vision; to see music bring hope to our local community. The volume of financial donors, organization partners, and event sponsors we have acquired is telling of the charisma and unity with which we operate. Needless to say, meetings are fun, exciting and full of laughter. If you would like to get involved, please contact info@melodyofhope.org
