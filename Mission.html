Our Beliefs

• We believe that we can help eradicate the issues that plague our local communities

• We believe that these concerts will attract volunteers, educate donors, create sponsorship opportunities, showcase artist on a charitable platform, fuel our marketing campaign, and most importantly, give our community an opportunity to help support a greater cause

• We believe in local artists who are the heart and soul of the music community. Our platform would help give them exposure and publicity from the diverse mix of talent and attendees at the concerts

• We believe that when the human heart is moved by music, the world hears. Change starts with a single melody of hope

• We believe in our foundational purpose, which is built upon the melody of the Gospel that gives a hopeful future to all
